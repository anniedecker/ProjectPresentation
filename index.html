Our Project

We chose to make a random seat generator using JSON.

We liked the challenge of the conditional statements on this particular project.

Conditionals:

  • Not allowing two students to sit together.
  • Sitting a certain student at a particular workstation.
  • Excluding a broken workstation from being assigned a student.
  • Lastly, whether or not to skip students.
